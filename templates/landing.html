About ISKCON Virar

Spreading Lord Krishna's message since 2010

ISKCON Virar is a branch of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) founded by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada in 1966. Our temple in Virar serves as a spiritual oasis for devotees and visitors seeking peace, wisdom, and divine connection.

We conduct regular devotional programs, distribute spiritual literature, and provide vegetarian prasadam to all visitors. Our mission is to propagate Krishna consciousness as explained in the Bhagavad-gita and Srimad Bhagavatam.
